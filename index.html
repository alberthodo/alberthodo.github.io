Human First | Designer | Engineer

Hodo is a human first engineer and designer who explores experimental interfaces, based on research in nature. His work ranges from developing software to prototyping pre-colonial futuristic technologies like AfroTechnoHistory. He has a degree in Computer Science from Ashesi University and a masters in Design from UC Berkeley’s College of Engineering.

XR & HARDWARE PROTOTYPING

Quantum-XR

For MIT Reality Hack 2024, my team was interested in Quantum-XR. It is an open source citizen science concept platform that allows the scientific community to collaborate with XR enthusiasts to create simulations of quantum physics phenomena in controlled environments. We focused on a proof of concept for simulating quantum properties.

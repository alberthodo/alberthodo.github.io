OVERVIEW

I had a friend who was very susceptible to falling sick due to underlying health issues. She had many devices to monitor her vitals and always needed extra care and attention. This motivated me to design a system to make it easier to manage.

I put together a team and led the end-to-end mobile experience to allow her to depend less on other people and provide a seamless way for her Doctors to monitor her always.

HIGHLIGHTS

Key Features Of The System

A secure
and easy hub to scan and monitor your health

01. Monitor your health with one click and an easy data storage for your doctor.

A smart companion
that improves your health with Machine Learning powered tips

02. Categorize data for your doctors and enjoy personalized tips.

Speak
with a doctor or have an emergency team on standby

03. In an emergency, speak with a doctor or request for an ambulance.

Supporting Features

04. Onboarding and profile creation sections to make the system smooth.

05. Simple pairing mode and health tips sections.

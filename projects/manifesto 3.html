Back to the Earth

In a world where designs, technologies and materials look strange, harm the environment, contributes
to climate change and diseases, we need to take a stand for change.

This manifesto challenges current eurocentric design norms and hints at nature and biomimicry as an alternative.
All the images were AI generated with OpenAI's DALL-E 1 to furthur stress the point that our emerging
technologies and design trends can play a part in learning from nature and designing with nature as well.

Dec 2022 [2 weeks]
Exhibited at the 2022 Jacobs Institute of Design Winter Showcase.

Mycorrhizae

Our connection with the environment fades even more with the progress of industralization. We become less conscious of how our technologies and practices harm the environment. What if we could remind ourselves that nature is alive and we are a part of nature? This project attempts to make the invisible tangible and visible, in the hopes of encouraging people to appreciate the beauty and complexity of nature and our relationship to it. Mycorhizae is an interactive exhibit highlighting the hidden communication between plants, mushrooms and the underground forest ecosystem, known as the Mycorhizal network.

Inspired by Netflix's Fantastic Fungi series. Mycelium has collective consciousness and provides a wel hidden communication between plants, mushrooms and the underground forest ecosystem, known as the Mycorrhizal network. It has many properties but one fascinating thing it that it works similarly to the World Wide Web [WWW] and internet as we know.

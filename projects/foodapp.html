Chakula

Chakula is an application designed specifically for the Ashesi community, enabling individuals to conveniently place orders at any of the campus cafeterias.

The primary goal behind its creation was to minimize the waiting time experienced by people at the different cafeterias .
Ashesi students wasted several hours on average a day at the school cafeteria because of the long queues and they had no reliable means of knowing what meals are available until it was their turn to order.

This project seeks to streamlines the process whether they prefer dining in, picking up their orders, or opting for delivery.

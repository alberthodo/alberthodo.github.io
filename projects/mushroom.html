Mycorrhizae

Mycorhizae is an interactive exhibit highlighting the hidden communication between
plants, mushrooms and the underground forest ecosystem, known as the
Mycorhizal network.

Inspired by Netflix's Fantastic Fungi series. Mycelium has collective consciousness
and provides a wel hidden communication between plants, mushrooms and the underground forest
ecosystem, known as the Mycorrhizal network.
It has many properties but one fascinating thing it that it works similarly to the
World Wide Web [WWW] and internet as we know.


This project seeks to create stronger connections between humans and nature
and help perple persive nature as a living entity

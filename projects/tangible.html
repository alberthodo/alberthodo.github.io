Dynamic Tangible Interfaces

Digital devices are dynamic in nature. They serve multiple purposes with different apps
and are designed to keep only the important data in sight. What if we could bring that
dynamic nature to hardware devices? What wouold they look like? What new interaction
techniques would be discovered?

We explored 3 simple scenarios;
1. A dynamic light switch that only appears when you are near it.
2. A dynamic TV remote that shows basic buttons and hides other on demand.
3. An on demand signage that can be updated and animated instantly in emergencies


This project explores inflatable interfaces and speculates what smart IoT interfaces could be at the home
and public spaces. A series of 3 basic interfaces were prototyped for an exhibition.

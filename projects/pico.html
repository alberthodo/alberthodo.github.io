Pico Too Can Dance

Pico is a lovely 2D robot dog who enjoys dancing in the maker studio / fabrication lab when he hears the sound of music.

Fun | 1 day | Fabrication

Brief

Pets and robots

The aim of this project was to create a 2D pet for the design maker space to cheer people up and keep them company. Good design sometimes needs to be fun, lo-fi and rough in nature but with a dash of personality and charm to enhace creativity.
When moral is low due to tight deadlines, a mechanical pet might be all we need.

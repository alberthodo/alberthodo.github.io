Vitz Health Companion

I grew up in a home with a friend who has special health needs. She has a compromized immune system and
has to record her health data regurlarly.We also needed the constant attention of health professionals.
She has several devices to monitor various health conditions and she hates documenting them on paper or an
excel file multiple times daily.

Vitz companion is a health buddy I designed to help cater for her health needs regularly by tracking her data
on one device and providing easy access to doctors..

It has 3 main components that make it unique.
- It gives her personalized lifestyle tips.
- It allows her to call her doctor or an ambulance when in emergencies
- It gives her control of all her health devices on one interface.

This project took over a year and has undergone 3 life cycles. It has been designed from the ground
up each time. It is currently being developed.

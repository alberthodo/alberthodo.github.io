Albert Senyo Hodo.

I enhance human experiences, though experimental design and research, based on attributes found in nature. I use a blend of software and hardware to prompt people to reconsider humanities place on this earth. Taking an approach of questioning larger systems at play, I present ideas as more palpable and practical

I have experiences working with and collaborationg with agencies such as Meta , MasterCard Foundation, Jacobs Institute of Design, KnackApp, Exploratorium , etc. I have a unique background that allows me to explore interface design and prototype ideas through software programming, IoT Tinkering and New Media Design.

I enjoy building interactive pieces, designing intuitive user interfaces, programming soft robotics and 3D systems. I am open to new opportunities and meeting new people, kindly reach out if you are interested to know about my work.
